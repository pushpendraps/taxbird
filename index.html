About Us

Welcome to the About Us section of our law firm's website, founded by Pushpendra Singh. We are a team of highly experienced legal professionals, dedicated to providing our clients with comprehensive legal solutions for their business needs. Our firm offers a wide range of services including management, compliance, and advisory services to support businesses across various industries.Our management services are designed to help businesses with their day-to-day operations. We offer bookkeeping services to help businesses maintain accurate financial records, payroll management services to ensure timely payment of employee salaries and planning services to help businesses achieve their long-term financial goals.In addition to management, we also offer compliance services to help businesses comply with legal and regulatory requirements. Our compliance services include tax planning and filing, GST filing, corporate documentation, company incorporation, MSME/Udyam registration, IEC registration, and FSSAI registration. We understand the importance of compliance and work diligently to ensure that our clients stay compliant with all relevant regulations.Our advisory services are designed to help businesses make informed decisions about their operations. We offer reporting services to help businesses track their financial performance and provide recommendations for improvement. Additionally, we offer capital fundraising services to help businesses secure the necessary capital to achieve their growth goals.At our law firm, we prioritize our clients' success above all else. We take the time to understand each client's unique business needs and work tirelessly to provide tailored legal solutions that meet those needs. Our team of legal professionals has a wealth of experience and knowledge, which we leverage to provide high-quality legal services to our clients.In summary, if you are looking for a reliable and experienced law firm that can provide comprehensive legal solutions for your business needs, look no further than our firm. Our founder, Pushpendra Singh, and the rest of our team are committed to providing the highest quality legal services to our clients. Contact us today to learn more about how we can help your business succeed.
